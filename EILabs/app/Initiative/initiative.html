Arz kia hai

Arz kia hai is a community driven initiative of writers which stands for shayars and fans. The aim of our community is to prepare as well as enhance the skill and creativity of our upcoming writers without focusing on any of their gender, cast, creed, language and social status. A change in perspective will not only give the chance to privileged but will also help the talented, skilled, non privileged people who limitise their capabilities and will act as a helping hand to bring them in the world of literature.. This will encourage influence of literature in lower and deprived section of the society.


Arz Kia Hai - launched in 2013, was used to be full - fledged first nationwide community initiative for young writers. Started from city of Taj, to very few members has grown over 100+ writers, 10k+ readerbase, 8 dedicated strong communities in several cities to its presence all over the nation.

Mimamasa Bastar

Mimamasa Bastar is a volunteer driven initiative and organisation which presents its views independently over whats going on in bastar and will present investigations for development of awareness and future of bastar.


Mimamsa Bastar -launched after sukma attack, aims at Investigating the future Bastar through its 5 principles of Investigation. Followed by a major survey, which revealed the truth of Bastar, its receiving over-whelming response of people with applications for becoming its volunteer to help frame the future of this deprived yet very rich location in our Nation.

TEDx Pandri

TEDx was an initiative by Poetcard impression. We were surrounded by people who highly doubted the success of the event but we were not just a group of blokes who wanted an adventure, we were a team ready to start a trend.


TEDx was basically created in the spirit of TED’s mission of ideas worth spreading. Which supports independent organizers who want to create TED like event in their community. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized people that brings people together to share TED like experience.
